Neal studied music at The University of North Texas, Northern Arizona University, and Texas Wesleyan University. These studies culminated in his recipiency of a minor in music from The University of Texas at Arlington.

The instruments he has studied include acoustic guitar, electric guitar, Classical guitar, bass, banjo, mandolin, piano, harmonica, cello, and fiddle. Over the course of his college career he participated in seven choir ensembles, and five Classical guitar ensembles. He has founded several performing bands over the last ten years. He is currently performing in Chillamundo. They released their debut EP Radio Is On in September of 2017.

He began teaching music at the age of fifteen. His students have gone on to form bands, record full-length albums, and enjoy pickin' on their back porch.

If you are at all interested in learning to play an instrument or write a song, Neal can take you from “Ow, my fingers hurt!” to performing on stage and beyond. If you have read this far, please follow this inquiry link to recieve a free thirty-minute introductory lesson.
