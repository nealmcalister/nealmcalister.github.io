Neal McAlister is a teacher, musician, and writer. He currently instructs guitar, bass, vocals, and band coaching at School of Rock in Mansfield. He also works as a freelance writer, web designer, and content editor.

He enjoys songwriting, recording, engineering, and learning any instrument he can get his hands on. He is also an avid coffee drinker. He enjoys reading everything from post-colonial poetry to dystopian science fiction.

Neal is a father of one. He and his daughter enjoy feeding the ducks, employing their imaginations, going on walks, and reading Dr. Seuss books.

Neal received a Bachelor of Arts in English with a minor in music from The University of Texas at Arlington in 2015. He lives in Arlington, Texas.
